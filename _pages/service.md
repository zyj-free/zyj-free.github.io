## Social Service and Leadership

Served as the class learning committee member, responsible for coordinating academic affairs, organizing class activities, communicating course-related information, and collecting feedback from classmates. This role helped strengthen class cohesion and supported the smooth operation of daily academic work.

Served as a minister in the Library Student Management Committee, where I participated in departmental organization and student service work. I also delivered multiple formal reports and presentations, contributing to the management and communication of committee activities.

Participated in volunteer service for the Challenge Cup and marathon events. My annual volunteer service time exceeded **150 hours**, reflecting a sustained commitment to campus service, public engagement, and social responsibility.
