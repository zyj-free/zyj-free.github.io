Selected Projects
======
**Dots and Boxes AI System via Deep RL & MCTS** | *Dec 2024 – Jul 2025*
- Developed a self-play iterative framework integrating **Deep Reinforcement Learning** and **Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS)** to solve sparse reward issues.
- Implemented endgame theorems, reverse training architecture, and hash storage to accelerate strategy convergence.
- **Outcome**: Won **National 1st Prize**, China Computer Game Championship (Top tier national competition).

**Edge-ANNoy: Lightweight Search Algorithm for Edge Devices** | *Apr 2025 – Sep 2025*
- Proposed **Edge-ANNoy**, utilizing a dual-anchor partitioning strategy to reduce index space complexity from $O(d \cdot N)$ to $O(N)$.
- Achieved **85.5% recall** with **30x faster retrieval** than HNSW under 512MB memory limit; maintained >70% recall even under extreme 16MB constraints.
- **Outcome**: **1 Invention Patent** (Pending); Co-authored paper and patent documentation.

**Precision Irrigation System with Multi-modal Sensors & Vision** | *Dec 2024 – Jun 2025*
- Engineered an intelligent irrigation system fusing **multi-modal flexible sensors** (soil moisture) and **YOLOv8** (crop growth recognition, 92.7% accuracy).
- Enabled real-time decision-making via low-latency ICN network (≤3ms) and cloud-based RL engine for second-level water/fertilizer dispatch.
- **Outcome**: Won **National Special Prize** (TRIZ Cup), **National Bronze** (China International College Students' Innovation Competition), and **National 2nd Prize** ("Challenge Cup"); **1 Utility Model Patent** (Pending).
